# Fix VPN Connection Errors

## Summary

Use this article when the VPN does not connect or disconnects after you sign in.

## Audience

Employees who use a company laptop to connect to internal tools.

## Before You Start

Make sure you have:

- A working internet connection
- Your company username and password
- Access to your multi-factor authentication method
- The approved VPN application installed

## Symptoms

You may see one of these issues:

- The VPN stays on "Connecting."
- The VPN says your sign-in failed.
- The VPN disconnects after a few seconds.
- Internal websites do not load after the VPN connects.

## Steps

### 1. Check your internet connection

Open a public website, such as a news site or search page.

If the page does not load, reconnect to Wi-Fi or restart your router.

### 2. Restart the VPN application

1. Close the VPN application.
2. Wait 10 seconds.
3. Open the VPN application again.
4. Try to connect.

### 3. Confirm your username and password

Sign in using your full company email address.
